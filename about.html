About Us

At just 13 years old, Malak, an ambitious and creative project owner, has embarked on an extraordinary venture in the world of fashion. Her brainchild, "Angel accessories dz" is a one-of-a-kind collection that blends youthful energy with the luxurious feel of satin silk. Malak's keen eye for design and passion for textiles have led her to curate a line of vibrant, textured silk fabrics that bring a playful twist to the world of fashion. With each piece carefully crafted, Malak not only showcases her entrepreneurial spirit at a remarkably young age but also introduces a fresh and exciting perspective to the realm of satin silk fashion.
